Client Checkin

In order to update this doc please go to https://github.com/10gen/corp/edit/master/www/templates/clienthub/docs/checkin.html

Once you have completed this doc please send an email to thameka@10gen.com and jackie@10gen.com. The email should contain a link to this doc and notify us that the customer check-in occured.

Pre-requisites:

  • Familiarize yourself with the client: Review most recent notes, onboarding form
  • Check with other engineers if this is a new customer for you
  • Check most recent JIRA tickets BEFORE calling
  • Check in with account manager ({{client.display_account_contact()}}) to coordinate communications

Goals are:

  • Check in with every commercial support customer
  • Maintain our relationship as a trusted advisor
  • Get a temperature reading of their use and deployment of MongoDB
