Onboarding Checklist

Pre-requisites:

  • Check most recent JIRA tickets BEFORE calling

    • Purpose of this call:This call is an introduction to the 10gen engineering/support team and serves two primary purposes. First, to record a high-level overview of the customer's application architecture and, specifically, their MongoDB cluster. During this process, you should attempt to identify any high-level problems (e.g. using version 2.0.1 when 2.0.4 is available) and encourage the client to get their details in MMS. The second purpose is to ensure that the customer is familiar with our support infrastructure and best practices for requesting help during business hours (https://jira.mongodb.org/browse/CS) and after-hours (+1 646-201-9247 / +44 (0)20 300 49321).

    In order to update this doc please go to https://github.com/10gen/corp/blob/master/www/templates/clienthub/docs/onboarding.html

    Once you have completed this doc please send an email to support@10gen.com. The email should contain a link to this doc and notify the support team that the customer has been onboarded.

    You should also add the customer to the Hot Sites page on the wiki (https://wiki.10gen.com/display/cs/Hot+Sites) if they require extra attention.
